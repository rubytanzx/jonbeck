ABOUT

This is Jon Beck.

Jon Beck is an international award winning photographer, specializing in fine art, actor portraits, landscape and building photography. Jon’s approach to his work is one of no compromise, from image capture, printing and framing he uses only the finest quality materials available. All Jon’s images are captured on professional film using medium format equipment. Jon’s stunning art pieces decorate the walls of many homes, offices and commercial spaces around Australia and the world.

“I am drawn to the diversity of the world – from Australia’s red soil centre, the character in peoples faces and the unique textures of our building heritage. My combined passion for the outdoors , people and their environment enables me to create iconic images from Australia’s diverse landscape, New Zealand and the world.I believe photograph’s have to provoke an emotion, represent uniqueness and tell a story.”

Living in Queensland, he is surrounded by beautiful beaches, the unique characters of its people and ideal weather; which Jon uses for inspiration to create his stunning images. Jon constantly strives to capture spectacular light and mood within his images, while maintaining a fresh hipster perspective. He believes a photograph has to provoke an emotion, represent the landscape and tell a story- but with a difference.
